Create a new bot

Step 2: Register a new bot with GroupMe

1. Go to dev.groupme.com/bots and log in with your standard GroupMe account

2. Click "Create Bot"

3. Select the group your bot will go in

4. (Optional) If you want your bot to have a custom avatar, right-click (Long Press on Mobile) on any image (from Google, GroupMe, etc.) and click "Copy image URL". Paste this into Avatar URL.

5. Put this URL as the callback URL. This will allow anyone in the group to type "/bot" to get information about the bot.
